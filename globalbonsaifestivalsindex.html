Global Bonsai Festivals

The 100th Kokufu-ten Bonsai Exhibition, the world's most prestigious bonsai event, was helt at the Tokyo Metropolitan Art Museum in Ueno Park, Tokyo,Japan. From Asia to Europe, Africa to the Americas, and across Oceania, the art of bonsai has grown into a truly global tradition. The Art of Bonsai is a comprehensive recourse for bonsai enthusiasts, offering a wealth of information and teqniques.
